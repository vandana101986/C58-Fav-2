My Favorite Dish
OBBATTU

Obbattu is also called as holige, bele obbattu or puran poli. It is basically a sweet flat bread recipe prepared mainly with all purpose flour with chana dal and jaggery stuffing. It is one of the popular sweet recipes, prepared especially during the festival seasons or during religious ceremony.
